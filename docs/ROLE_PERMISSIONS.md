# Role Permissions

## Citizen

- Register and log in.
- Submit complaints with typed text, evidence link, uploaded evidence, or voice note.
- View only their own complaints.
- Track assigned office, due date, status, and official responses.
- Receive notifications.
- Rate resolved complaints.
- Update their profile.

## Administrative Staff

- Log in only when active and assigned to an office.
- View only complaints routed to their office.
- Start review, ask for more information, resolve, or escalate cases.
- Write official responses.
- View office-level reports and office notifications.

## Admin

- View all complaints in the sector.
- Manage users, roles, account status, and staff office assignment.
- Manage categories, responsible offices, SLA days, priorities, and routing rules.
- Run SLA escalation checks.
- Print or download complaint reports.
- View audit logs.

## Public / Anonymous

- Track a complaint using a tracking number.
- Submit anonymous reports without an account or stored identity.
